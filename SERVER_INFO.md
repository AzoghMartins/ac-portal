# Welcome to Kardinal WoW

Wrath of the Lich King (3.3.5a) realm tuned for long-form, story-friendly play. Slow, deliberate pacing with tools to keep the world alive even when population is low.

## Quick Facts
- Expansion: WotLK 3.3.5a
- Focus: Immersive, slower progression; solo-capable, group-first vibe
- Realm uptime: Online 24/7
- Mods you’ll notice: Individual Progression, PlayerBots, Auction House Bot, Challenge Modes

## Core Features
- **Individual Progression**: You’re bound to the era you’re in until you beat its end boss/quest. Your Armory shows which wars you’ve cleared.
- **PlayerBots**: Party-ready bots (MultiBot compatible) to fill dungeons and make the world feel populated. Command list on the PlayerBot wiki.
- **Auction House Bot**: Keeps key goods available without wrecking the economy.
- **Era-accurate pacing**: XP/loot matches the era you’re in; no surprise boosts or skips.

## Challenge Modes
Enable at the **Shrine of Challenge** near each starting-zone graveyard (level 1; DKs at 55). You can stack most modes unless they conflict:
- **Hardcore**: Death is permanent (ghost forever).
- **Self-Crafted**: Only wear what you craft.
- **Normal Gear Only**: Only Normal/Poor quality gear.
- **Slow XP Gain**: XP at 0.5×.
- **Very Slow XP Gain**: XP at 0.25×.
- **Quest XP Only**: XP only from quests.

## How to Join
1) Register on the portal: https://kardinalwow.duckdns.org/login?mode=register
2) Download the WotLK 3.3.5a client (and optionally the HD Patch for 3.3.5a): https://www.chromiecraft.com/en/downloads/
3) Set your realmlist to `kardinalwow.duckdns.org` in `Data/enUS/realmlist.wtf`.
4) Log in with the username/password you registered on the portal.
